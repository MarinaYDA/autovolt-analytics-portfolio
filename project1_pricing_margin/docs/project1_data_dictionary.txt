Project 1 – Pricing & Margin Analysis (clean dataset)
File: project1_pricing_margin_clean.csv

Each row = one completed job at a fictional hybrid-battery service company.

Columns:
- job_id (int): Unique identifier for the job.
- job_date (datetime): Date of the job.
- vehicle_brand (str): Vehicle brand (Toyota, Honda, Ford, etc.).
- vehicle_model (str): Specific hybrid model.
- region (str): Region of the job (North, South, East, West).
- job_type (str): Type of job ("Install", "Diagnostic", "Warranty_Replacement").
- battery_type (str): Battery category ("NiMH_Standard", "NiMH_Premium", "LiIon_Standard", "LiIon_Premium").
- is_mobile (int 0/1): Whether the job was mobile service.
- parts_cost (float): Cost of parts in USD.
- labor_hours (float): Labor hours spent on the job.
- hourly_rate (int): Labor hourly rate in USD.
- travel_cost (float): Travel cost for mobile jobs.
- discount_pct (float): Discount percentage applied (0–0.2).
- total_price (float): Total price charged to customer.
- margin (float): total_price minus all costs (parts + labor + travel).
- customer_id (int): Synthetic customer identifier.
- is_repeat_customer (int 0/1): Whether the customer is a repeat customer.
